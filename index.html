Why to Chooose Nomadica AI?

Nomadica AI is a travel assistant designed to make your journeys smoother and more enjoyable. Whether you're looking for the best restaurants, need help navigating a new city, or want to book an eco-friendly accommodation, our app is here to assist you every step of the way.

How to use?

Download and Install the App

- Start by downloading Nomadica AI from the Google Play Store or the app's official website.
- Once the download is complete, install the app on your device by following the on-screen instructions.

Log In

- Open the app and simply login with your Google Account.
- If already registered to Application no need to login.

Explore the Main Features

- The home screen provides access to all the main features of Nomadica AI, such as Search, Booking, Voice Assistant, Emergency Services, and more.
- Use the Search feature to find destinations, accommodations, and e-vehicle options.
- The Booking option allows you to reserve accommodations or e-vehicles by selecting your destination and preferred service.

Utilize the AI-Powered Voice Assistant

- Activate the voice assistant by tapping the microphone icon or using the wake word if enabled.
- Speak your request, such as “Find hotels near me,” “Book an e-vehicle to the airport,” or “What’s the weather like in Delhi?”
- The voice assistant will respond to your queries and guide you through the process.

Emergency Services and SOS Activation

- Access emergency services by clicking on the Emergency Services button on the home screen.
- Use the SOS Button to automatically send an emergency alert with your location to your pre-selected contacts.
- You can also call specific emergency services (Police, Ambulance, Fire) directly from the app by selecting the respective option.

Customize Settings

- Navigate to the settings menu to customize the app according to your preferences.
- Adjust notification settings, language preferences, and update your emergency contact information if needed.

Log Out or Switch Users

- When you’re done using the app, log out from the account section if needed.
- The app also allows switching between multiple user profiles if more than one person uses the device.
